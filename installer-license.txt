XFB - Radio Automation Software
The open-source solution for professional radio broadcasting, rebuilt with Qt6.

Developed by Frederic Bogaerts at Netpack Online Solutions (https://www.netpack.pt).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT XFB DOES

  - Database management for music, jingles, advertisements, and programs
  - Automated scheduling for playback, ad slots, and program airing
  - Drag & drop playlist building from your music library
  - Main player plus two auxiliary LP players for DJ mixing
  - Per-player 10-band equalizer and broadcast-style compressor (requires ffmpeg)
  - Real-time 432 Hz playback and batch conversion
  - Live program recording
  - Icecast/Shoutcast and .m3u/.pls stream client with automatic reconnect
  - Torrent search and download via Tor-routed onion sites
  - Accessibility: screen reader support, keyboard navigation, audio feedback
  - Cross-platform: macOS, Linux (Debian/Arch), and Windows
